## Shadow type, energy & forms rework:
### Shadow Type & Energy:
- Added a new Shadow type which is super effective against every other type (except shadow itself 0.5x)
- Every Type hits Shadow for neutral damage (except shadow itself 0.5x)
- Shadow Pokémon retain their original STAB, despite losing their original typing
- Shadow Energy now requires your Pokémon to have 250+ friendship to swap forms
- Shadow Energy now gets consumed on use
### Shadow Lugia changes:
- It now has the same stats, abilities and BST as regular Lugia
- Abilities are called Shadow Pressure and Shadow Guard but that's just for _flair_
### Shadow Mewtwo changes:
- Has +20% offensive & -20% defensive stats
- Custom Ability - Shadow Boost (boosts super effective moves by 1.25x)
## In relation to ATM x MSD:
- It will stay fully compatible as before
- Code for Armored/Shadow Mewtwo has been ported over but the new model isn't ready yet
- Since I am not allowed to port over the old models from ATM x MSD, I decided to leave them in there for now
- Meaning you have to have ATM x MSD installed for Shadow/Armored Mewtwo & the Shadow Megas.
